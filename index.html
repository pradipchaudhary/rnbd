RNBD • A Living Memorial

RNBD (Ram Narayan & Bhatinbati Devi) is a heartfelt digital shrine preserving the legacy of two remarkable souls. From cherished photographs to handwritten letters, everything here is curated with love by their grandson, Pradip Chaudhary, so future generations can feel their warmth and wisdom.

  • Honour enduring love and resilience
  • Preserve stories, wisdom & cherished memories
  • Accessible anywhere, for everyone, forever
